# Chapter 8. Carbon Stocks and Inventory

## 8.1 What this chapter delivers

This chapter quantifies the carbon stock held in the mangrove and tidal salt marsh ecosystems at the four study landscapes, against IPCC 2013 Wetlands Supplement Tier 2 standards and Verra VM0033 v2.1 methodological requirements. It is the chapter at which the Carbon Inventory Report being finalised by the carbon team is integrated into the Final Report. The structural skeleton, the section sequence, and the cross-references to the rest of the Final Report are fixed here. The numerical content will be inserted at the named CARBON_PENDING blocks once the Carbon Inventory Report is delivered, on the schedule named in Chapter 1 §1.5.

## 8.2 Carbon pools assessed

The chapter reports on the four carbon pools that the IPCC Tier 2 framework requires for tidal wetlands: above-ground biomass (AGB), below-ground biomass (BGB), deadwood, and soil organic carbon (SOC). The methods for each pool are summarised in Chapter 4 §4.2 and documented at full depth in the Carbon Inventory Report (§3).
